Welcome

The Medex College School of Nursing and Health Professions is committed to the development of students as individuals and leaders striving for excellence in inter-professional practice and the promotion of healthy communities. The School of Nursing and Health Professions offers Diploma programs in Nursing, Pharmacy, Medical laboratory technology, Physical Therapy, Health care giving. The School of Nursing and Health Professions supports the mission, goals and policies of the College. All programs are committed to the development of healthcare professionals with visionary leadership, demonstrating excellence in critical thinking, communication and technical skills.  Nursing students enter a program that is grounded in standards-based practice and holistic care, with focused intensive classroom and clinical experiences in a variety of nursing areas such as elderly care, acute care, community health, and specialized care (Dementia care, Alzheimer’s care and Palliative care). All programs are contained a class room based theory, clinical practice sessions and hospital based clinical practice. This combination enhances the success for our students.

Vision

A healthy world through health education.

Mission

Our mission is to foster the growth, development and educational achievement of all students by promoting health and wellness, in a safe and supportive environment. We are committed to fulfill the insufficiency of health care providers, collaboration with public and private agencies and health care organizations to promote and protect the health and well-being of the diverse communities throughout Sri Lanka, the nation and the world.

Values

Elimination of health disparities
Community engagement and service
Social and public health responsibility and ethics
Delivering quality lecturing, training and services
Promoting teamwork and collaboration
Respecting organizational tradition while encouraging innovation Direct guidance for the job opportunities

The Medex College School of Nursing and Health Professions focus on these strategic values enables the School to fulfill its stated mission of providing quality education, research, service and leadership aimed at improving public health, eliminating health disparities and fulfill the highest demand of the health related job opportunities locally, nationally, globally.
